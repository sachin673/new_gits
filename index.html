ABOUT CONFERENCE:

The 2023 International Conference on Electrical, Electronics, Communication and Computers (ELEXCOM’23), takes place for the first time in Roorkee (Haridwar), situated on the right bank of the Ganges river, at the foothills of the Shivalik ranges. Haridwar is regarded as a holy place for Hindus, hosting important religious events and serving as a gateway to several prominent places of worship.

ELEXCOM'23 offers a special chance to connect, engage, and influence the development of autonomous vehicles, worldwide. Details may be found at (website link) and related links.

The central theme of ELEXCOM’23 is four-fold: Applications of Autonomous vehicle in Electrical, Electronics, Communication and Computer engineering. National and international organisations, agencies, industries, and governments seek to define roadmaps for autonomous vehicle expectations, technological criteria, and standards that are required for full use and integration. The next generation of Autonomous vehicles will be used for a wide spectrum of civilian and public domain applications.

Special attention will be placed on research opportunities and 'what comes next' in terms of the critical technologies that must be used to progress the state-of-the-art. The new, innovative aspect of ELEXCOM’23 is that it will include for the first time a Drone workshop.

Through plenary talks, invited papers, and presentations, the conference will produce a comprehensive knowledge of what the industry, society, national, and international authorities need and what the necessary next steps are before autonomous vehicles are used in routine applications.
